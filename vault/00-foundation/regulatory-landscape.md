# Regulatory landscape (employment AI)

Primary-source facts behind [governance/regulatory-readiness](../../governance/regulatory-readiness.md).
**Retrieved & verified 2026-06-20. Not legal advice** — confirm current obligations with counsel.

## EU AI Act — employment is high-risk

Annex III, **Point 4** ("Employment, workers management and access to self-employment") designates
as high-risk:
- **4(a)** AI for *"the recruitment or selection of natural persons, in particular to place
  targeted job advertisements, to analyse and filter job applications, and to evaluate candidates."*
- **4(b)** AI for work-related decisions: promotion, termination, task allocation, and monitoring
  performance/behaviour.

High-risk obligations (Annex III systems) become applicable **2 August 2026** (Article 113).
Sources: [Annex III](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/annex/3/) ·
[implementation timeline](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/implementation-timeline/).

## NYC Local Law 144 — AEDTs

Applies to Automated Employment Decision Tools used for hiring/promotion of NYC candidates (incl.
NYC-linked remote roles). Requires: an **annual independent bias audit** with **publicly published**
results (adverse-impact + methodology); **candidate notice ≥ 10 business days** before AEDT use, with
an opt-out path. Enforced by NYC DCWP; civil penalties **$500–$1,500 per violation/day**; enforcement
began **5 July 2023**.
Sources: DCWP [AEDT FAQ / guidance](https://www.nyc.gov/assets/dca/downloads/pdf/about/DCWP-AEDT-FAQ.pdf)
(final rule adopted Apr 2023) · [Deloitte summary](https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/services/audit-assurance/articles/nyc-local-law-144-algorithmic-bias.html).

## GDPR Article 22 — automated decisions

*"The data subject shall have the right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated
processing, including profiling, which produces legal effects concerning him or her or similarly
significantly affects him or her."* Safeguards include the right to **human intervention**, to
express a view, and to contest the decision.
Source: [GDPR Art. 22](https://gdpr-info.eu/art-22-gdpr/).
