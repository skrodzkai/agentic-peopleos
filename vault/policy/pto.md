# Paid time off (PTO) policy

Salaried employees accrue PTO monthly and may carry over up to **5 days** year to year.
Negative balances require manager + People Ops approval.

> Canonical, human-approved policy. Agents may cite it (with this note's id and
> `last-reviewed` date) but never adjudicate an exception — anything outside it routes to the
> `exception-path` above. Balances are authoritative in the `source-of-record`, not here.
