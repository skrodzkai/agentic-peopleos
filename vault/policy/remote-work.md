# Remote work policy

Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval. Fully remote
arrangements require VP sign-off and a documented business case.

> This is a canonical, human-approved policy. Agents may cite it (with this note's id and
> `last-reviewed` date). Anything outside it escalates to a People Business Partner.
