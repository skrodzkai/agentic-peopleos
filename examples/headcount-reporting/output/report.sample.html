Metric definitions
  • Headcount — Active worker count at the as-of date (point-in-time), including employees on protected leave.
  • FTE — Full-time-equivalent count, weighting each assignment by scheduled hours against the region's full-time standard.
  • Net headcount growth — Change in active headcount over a period, defined as ending minus beginning (reconciles exactly).
  • Span of control — Average number of direct reports per people-manager (counts every manager, including managers of managers).
  • Span outlier rate — Share of people-managers whose span falls outside the target band (sub-scale or overloaded).
  • Management layers — Reporting depth from the top executive to individual contributors, reported as a distribution (not just the deepest chain).
  • Contingent-workforce ratio — Contractors as a share of the blended workforce.
  • Representation by level — Demographic-group share of the population at each level.
  • Leadership diversity — Demographic-group share among people-leaders / senior levels.
