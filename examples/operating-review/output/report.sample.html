Metric definitions
  • Headcount — Active worker count at the as-of date (point-in-time), including employees on protected leave.
  • FTE — Full-time-equivalent count, weighting each assignment by scheduled hours against the region's full-time standard.
  • Net headcount growth — Change in active headcount over a period, defined as ending minus beginning (reconciles exactly).
  • Span of control — Average number of direct reports per people-manager (counts every manager, including managers of managers).
  • Contingent-workforce ratio — Contractors as a share of the blended workforce.
  • Voluntary attrition — Annualized rate of employee-initiated departures.
  • Regrettable attrition — Annualized rate of voluntary departures the company would have preferred to keep (criteria-based, not manager discretion).
  • Total turnover rate — Annualized rate of all separations (voluntary + involuntary). The ISO 30414 headline turnover figure.
  • 12-month retention — Share of a matured hire cohort still employed 12 months later.
  • Case volume — Count of distinct People Ops cases opened in the period, also reported per capita.
  • SLA attainment — Share of SLA-due cases resolved within their SLA target, counting open-but-breached cases against the rate.
  • Time to resolution — Wall-clock elapsed time from case open to resolved.
  • Open-case backlog / aging — Open cases bucketed by age, with breached cases highlighted.
  • Leadership diversity — Demographic-group share among people-leaders / senior levels.
