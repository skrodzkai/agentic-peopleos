Methodology and controls

TSR = (ending 30-day simple average closing price + dividends during the period) / beginning 30-day simple average closing price. Payout interpolates linearly between stated points and is capped at 200%.

  • Comparator rule: S&P Software & Services-style synthetic index members present at both start and end.
  • Excluded from ranking: QEXQ, RYVQ.
  • Monte Carlo inputs: 5,000 paths, seed 7182026, 3.0 years, risk-free rate 3.80%, constant synthetic volatilities and correlations.
  • Monte Carlo precision: SE $2.14; 95% MC CI $97.39 to $105.76 per target share.
  • Valuation caveat: the Monte Carlo path ranks total return using terminal prices; it does not model daily 30-day averaging-window paths, post-performance settlement timing, forfeitures, or award-specific accounting policy.
  • Public-safety boundary: no real issuer, ticker, stock price, proxy, award, employee, or employer data is included.
  • This is not accounting advice, legal advice, tax advice, investment advice, or an auditor-approved valuation opinion.
