Qualitative review in scope · 6 factors

A Medium/High quantitative screen triggers ISS's qualitative review — the factors a committee should be ready to address
Second stage
  • Magnitude: CEO pay is a high multiple of the peer median — review absolute level.
  • Pay-for-performance: pay percentile outruns TSR percentile vs the peer group.
  • Financials: pay also outruns financial performance (EVA-style) — not just TSR.
  • Incentive design: goal rigor, metric choice, and payout curves vs market.
  • Problematic practices: single-trigger CIC, excessive perks, discretionary payouts.
  • Responsiveness: prior say-on-pay support and disclosed shareholder engagement.
