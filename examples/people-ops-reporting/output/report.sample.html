Metric definitions
  • Case volume — Count of distinct People Ops cases opened in the period, also reported per capita.
  • SLA attainment — Share of SLA-due cases resolved within their SLA target, counting open-but-breached cases against the rate.
  • Time to resolution — Wall-clock elapsed time from case open to resolved.
  • Reopen rate — Share of resolved cases that were reopened -- the guard against SLA gaming and premature closure.
  • First-contact resolution (FCR) — Share of resolved cases closed on the first touch with no follow-up.
  • Case CSAT — Satisfaction with resolved People Ops cases.
  • Open-case backlog / aging — Open cases bucketed by age, with breached cases highlighted.
